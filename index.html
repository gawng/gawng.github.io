My work

My Ph.D. research is on intelligent composites design for marine structures such as hydrofoils, propellers, turbines, struts, and energy saving and energy harvesting devices. Composites offer many benefits such as low structural weight, high strength-to-weight ratio, passive load adaptivity due to material anisotropy, reduced electromagnetic signature, corrosion resistance, and the potential for embedded fiber bragg grating sensors. They can also have improved fatigue life, enhanced cavitation performance, and reduced lifetime life-cycle cost if they are designed properly.
